1. <!DOCTYPE html >

        You'll need this tag of the beginning of every HTML document you creat.It ensure that a
        broswer know that it's reading HTML,and that expects HTML5,the latest version.
        Even though This isn't actually an HTML tag,it's still good one to know.

2. <html>

        This is another tag that tells a brower that it's reading HTML.The <html>tag gose 
        straight after the DOCTYPE tag,and you close it with a <html>tag right at the end of your
        file.Everthing else in your document gose between these tags.

3. <head>

        <head>tag starts the header section of your file.The stuff that gose in here doesn't
        appear on your webpage.Instead,it contains metadata for search engines,and info for 
        your brower.
